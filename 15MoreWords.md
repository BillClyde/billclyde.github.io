## Toki Pona: More Words

lukin
: look, see

wile
: want, need, have to, must

jaki
: dirty, disgusting, garbage

tomo
: house, indoors, building

ko
: soft or squishy substance, paste

ni
: this, that

mun
: moon, night sky, stars
