## Toki Pona: Interjections

pona!
: Great! Thanks! OK!

ike!
: oh no! thats wrong! that hurts!

pakala!
: damn!

mu
: moo, meow, wow, ruff...

musi!
: cool!

a
: ah! (suprise)

a a a
: ha ha ha (laughter)
