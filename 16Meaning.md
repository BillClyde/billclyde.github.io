## Toki Pona: Meaning

> In toki pona word meanings are quite broad

mi telo e mi.
: I am washing myself.

mi pona e tomo mi.
: I am fixing my house.

jan lili li wile e kili telo.
: The child wants fruit juice.

pipi li moku e ona.
: The bug bit him.

jan ni li pona lukin.
: That person is pretty.

sina ike lukin.
: You are ugly.

ko jaki
: poop

ko moku
: baby food

jaki!
: Yuck!
