## Toki Pona: Examples

> We can say many things

mi nasa!
: I am crazy!

mi olin sina!
: I love you!

jan utala li utala e ona li moli e ona.
: The soldier fought with them and killed them.

sina jan lili!
: You are a child!

jan suli li moku e telo nasa.
: The adults drink alcohol.

mi jo li jan olin.
: I have a girlfriend.

mi mute li moli
: We kill. **or** We die.

sina ike mute!
: You are very bad!

> Some words work as exclamations

ike!
: Thats wrong! Oh dear!

pona!
: Great! Ok! Yay!
