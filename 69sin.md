## Toki Pona: sin

> **sin** means "more" or "new"

mi wile e suwi sin!
: I want more cookies!

jan sin li kama ala kama?
: Are more people coming?

mi wili e tomo tawa sin!
: I want a new car!

kalama musi ni li sin ala!
: That music is old!
