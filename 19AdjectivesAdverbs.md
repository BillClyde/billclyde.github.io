## Toki Pona: More About Adjectives and Adverbs

> The meaning of compound words is obtained by grouping left to right.  
> Order is important.

jan utala
: a soldier

jan utala pona
: a good soldier

jan utala pona mute
: many good soldiers

jan pona utala
: a fighting friend

mi lukin pona
: I am good looking

mi pona lukin
: I am looking good
