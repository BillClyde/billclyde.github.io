## Toki Pona: Temperature

seli
: heat, warm, fire

lete
: cold, freezing

---

lete li lon
: It is cold (The cold is present)

seli li lon
: It is hot

mi lete
: I'm cold

mi pilin lete
: I feel cold

moku li lete
: The food is cold

o seli li moku!
: Warm the food!

mi wile e telo sewi pi lete mute
: I want a very cold Coke.

> This is a very good example of using **pi**
