## Toki Pona: Practice

pipi li moku e noka mi.
: The insect bit my foot

oko li lukin.
: The eye sees.

nena kute li kute.
: The ear hears.

insa mi li ike.
: My stomach is bad.

luka mi li pona lukin.
: My arms look good.

lawa sina li suli mute.
: Your head is very big.

linja lawa mi li jo e telo.
: My hair is wet.

mi jo ala li len luka.
: I don't have any gloves.
