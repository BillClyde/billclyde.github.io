## Toki Pona: ali

> **ali** means "all"
> **ali** can be an adjective or a noun

jan ali
: Everyone, all people

jan ali li moku.
: Everyone is eating

ma ali li pona.
: All countries are good.

mije ali li sama.
: All men are the same.

ali li pona.
: Everything is fine.

mi wile li ali!
: I want everything!
