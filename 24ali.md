## Toki Pona: ali

> **ali** means "all"
> **ali** can be an adjective or a noun

jan ali
<details>
<summary>Translation</summary>
Everyone, all people
</details>

---

jan ali li moku.
<details>
<summary>Translation</summary>
Everyone is eating
</details>

---

ma ali li pona.
<details>
<summary>Translation</summary>
All countries are good.
</details>

---

mije ali li sama.
<details>
<summary>Translation</summary>
All men are the same.
</details>

---

ali li pona.
<details>
<summary>Translation</summary>
Everything is fine.
</details>

---

mi wile li ali!
<details>
<summary>Translation</summary>
I want everything!
</details>
