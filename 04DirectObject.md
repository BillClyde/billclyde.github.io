## Toki Pona: Direct Objects

  >That which receives the action is preceded by **e**

  **{mi, sina} + {verb} + e + {object}**  
  **{noun} + li + {verb} + e + {object}**  

  mi moku e kili.  
  <details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  I eat the fruit.  
  </details>

  ---
  
  pipi li moku e moku. 
  <details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>
  Bugs eat the food.  
  </details>
