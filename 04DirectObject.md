## Toki Pona: Direct Objects

  >That which receives the action is preceded by **e**

  {mi, sina} + {verb} + e + {object}  
  {noun} + li + {verb} + e + {object}  

  mi moku e kili -> I eat the fruit  
  pipi li moku e moku -> Bugs eat the food
