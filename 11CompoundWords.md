## Toki Pona: Compound Words

> Many words that are expressed using one word in English are expressed using multiple words in toki pona.

ilo moku
: fork, spoon

ilo utala
: gun

jan pona
: friend

jan utala
: soldier

jan unpa
: prostitute, lover

jan olin
: boyfriend, girlfriend, spouse

jan suli
: adult

jan lili
: child

telo nasa
: alcoholic drink

mi mute
: us

ona mute
: them, they
