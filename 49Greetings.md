## Toki Pona: Greetings and Courtesy

toki!
: Hello!

suno pona!
: Good Day!

lape pona!
: Sleep well!
: Have a good night.

moku pona!
: Eat well!
: Have a good meal.

mi tawa!
: I am going(Goodbye)

tawa pona!
: Go well(Goodbye)

kama pona!
: Welcome

musi pona!
: Have fun!
