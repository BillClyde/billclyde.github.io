## Toki Pona: Simple Answers

> Suppose someone asks you:

sina musi ala musi?
: Are you having fun?

> Then the response could be:

musi
: Yes I am having fun

musi ala
: No I am not having fun

> Another example:

sina lukin ala lukin e soweli lili mute?
: Have you seen the hamster?

lukin
: Yes I have seen it

lukin ala
: No I have not seen it
