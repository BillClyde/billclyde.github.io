## Toki Pona: Tense

> There are no tenses

mi pona  
: I am good
: **or** I was good
: **or** I will be good

sina moku
: You eat
: **or** You will eat
: **or** You have eaten

pipi li moli
: The bug is dead
: **or** The bug will die
: **or** The bug has died
