## Toki Pona: Tense

> There are no tenses

mi pona  
: I am good
: **or** I was good
: **or** I will be good

sina moku
: You eat
: You will eat
: You have eaten

pipi li moli
: The bug is dead
: The bug will die
: The bug has died
