## Toki Pona: ala

> **ala** negates the previous word

mi musi ala.
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>
I am not having fun.
</details>

---

mi sona ala.
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>
I am not smart
</details>

---

jan ala
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>
Nobody
</details>

---

jan ala li toki.
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>
Nobody is speaking.
</details>

---

tomo sewi lin lon ma.
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>
There are no tall buildings in the fields.
</details>

---

mi jo ala e len.
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>
I have no clothes
</details>

> **ala** by itself means "nothing"

ala li jaki.
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>
Nothing is dirty.
</details>

---

ala li suli.
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>
Nothing is important.
</details>

---

ala li sama.
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>
Nothing is the same.
</details>
