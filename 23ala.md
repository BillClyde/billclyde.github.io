## Toki Pona: ala

> **ala** negates the previous word

mi musi ala.
: I am not having fun.

mi sona ala.
: I am not smart

jan ala
: Nobody

jan ala li toki.
: Nobody is speaking.

tomo sewi lin lon ma.
: There are no tall buildings in the fields.

mi jo ala e len.
: I have no clothes

> **ala** by itself means "nothing"

ala li jaki.
: Nothing is dirty.

ala li suli.
: Nothing is important.

ala li sama.
: Nothing is the same.
