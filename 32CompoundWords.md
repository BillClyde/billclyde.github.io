## Toki Pona: More Compound Words

ilo suno
: candle, lamp

jan sewi
: diety

nasin sewi
: religion

ma tomo
: city

tomo telo
: bathroom

jan lawa
: leader

---

jan sewi li olin
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

God is love
</details>

---

nasin sewi li mute
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

There are many religions
</details>

---

jan lawa li nasa
<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

The leader is crazy
</details>
