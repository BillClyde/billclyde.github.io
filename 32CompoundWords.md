## Toki Pona: More Compound Words

ilo suno
: candle, lamp

jan sewi
: diety

nasin sewi
: religion

ma tomo
: city

tomo telo
: bathroom

jan lawa
: leader

---

jan sewi li olin
: God is love

nasin sewi li mute
: There are many religions

jan lawa li nasa
: The leader is crazy
