## Toki Pona: o

jan Same o!
: Hey James!

---

jan Pue o, pipi li lon len sina!
: Hey Paul, there is a mosquito on your shirt!

---

jan Samanta o, mi olin e sina.
: Samantha, I love you.

---

jan Same o, sina pona tawa mi.
: James, I like you

---

mama meli o, sina pali mute mute!
: Mom, you work too much!
