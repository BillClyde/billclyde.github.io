## Toki Pona: o

jan Same o!
<detail>
<summary>Translation</summary>

</detail>

---

jan Pue o, pipi li lon len sina!
<detail>
<summary>Translation</summary>

</detail>

---

<detail>

</detail>

<detail>
<summary>Translation</summary>

<detail>

</detail>
